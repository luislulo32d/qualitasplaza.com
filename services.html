Coverage

We perform a comprehensive analysis of your website, focusing on the four essential factors that affect online business sales.

1.- Customer Experience (CX)

Ensure the right online experience to your customers and provide the treatment that enhances your brand the best. Win better and more customers. Avoid putting all your marketing effort at risk. Reverse the bad experiences of your customers and convert more clients!


2.- User Experience (UX)

When users interact with a web page it is essential that they live a rewarding and satisfying experience: the platform has to be easy to navigate, efficient and work properly to generate better results.

3.- Best eCommerce Practices

The requirements and codes of eCommerce are a key factor in the performance of the Online business. Make sure that your website offers the standards and facilities required by the client in order to meet their needs.

4.- Technical factors

We verify methodically hundreds of technical components of your website to ensure that everything works correctly. Thanks to the findings of our technical audit you can provide the best performance to prevent users from collapsing and leaving your business. Something so obvious that sometimes the best webmasters miss it.
